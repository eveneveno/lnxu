    Hi, I’m Linning! I recently obtained my PhD from CUHK, Multimedia Lab (MMLab). Before that, I also had the pleasure of collaborating with brilliant minds at CUHK(SZ), working on Statistics and Operations Research. My research interests include AI for Film (🎬) and City (🏙), with a particular focus on Neural Rendering and innovative Scene Representations for large-scale spaces and daily living spaces.

    I'm passionate about creating immersive spaces that highlight the natural beauty of our world and envision the spatial intelligence enriching our lives in the near future :)
