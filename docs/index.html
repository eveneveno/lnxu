    I’m a third year PhD student @CUHK, Multimedia Lab (MMLab). Before joining MMLab, I had a great pleasure working with great minds at CUHK(SZ) on Statistics, Machine Learning, Operation Research, and Optimization. I have broad interest in Machine Learning, with special focus on Computer Vision. My research topics include AI for movies (🎬) and city (🏙), with a recent focus on neural rendering for urban scenes :)
